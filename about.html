The Wallify Workspace

From Passion Project to IG Powerhouse

It started in a small shared apartment in 2021. The founders of 34Devs noticed a recurring problem: Instagram creators were struggling to maintain a consistent, high-quality aesthetic across their profiles without spending hours in complex design software.

Wallify was born from a simple script that automated wallpaper layouts. Today, it's a full-suite utility platform that handles everything from color palette generation to dynamic story templates.
