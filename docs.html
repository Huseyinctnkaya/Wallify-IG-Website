Quick Start Guide

Learn how to showcase your Instagram content on your Shopify store in less than 5 minutes.

1. Install the App

Install Wallify IG from the Shopify App Store. Once installed, you'll be redirected to the app dashboard where you'll see the "Setup Guide".

2. Connect Instagram

Click on the "Connect Account" button. You will be redirected to Instagram to authorize Wallify IG to access your basic account information and media.

Note: We only request read access to your media. We never post on your behalf or access private messages.
