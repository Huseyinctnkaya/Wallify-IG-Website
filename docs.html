Quick Start Guide

Learn how to showcase your Instagram content on your Shopify store in less than 5 minutes.

Wallify IG is designed to be the simplest yet most powerful way to bring your social content to your storefront. Following this guide will help you connect your accounts and display your first feed.

1. Installation

Adding Wallify IG to your Shopify store is a one-click process.

Step 1: App Store

Find Wallify IG in the Shopify App Store and click "Install".

Step 2: Permissions

Accept the requested permissions to allow the app to interact with your theme.

2. Connecting Instagram

Sync your feed securely using the official Meta API.

Process Overview:

  1. Navigate to the Connect tab in the dashboard.
  2. Click the Connect Instagram Account button.
  3. Login to your Instagram account in the pop-up window.
  4. Grant "Read-only" permissions for your media and profile.

info Pro Tip: Make sure your Instagram account is a Creator or Business account for best performance.

4. Analytics

Track engagement and sales directly from your Instagram feeds.

Our Analytics dashboard shows you how users interact with your feed. Key metrics include:

Feed Impressions

How many times your feed was loaded by unique visitors.

Click-Through Rate (CTR)

The percentage of users who clicked on an image to view products.

Top Performing Posts

Identify which Instagram posts are driving the most interest.

Conversion Tracking

Measure revenue generated from users who visited from the feed.
