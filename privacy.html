Privacy Policy

Latest Version

Last Updated: October 24, 2023

At Wallify IG, we believe privacy is a fundamental human right. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our services. We've designed this document to be as clear and readable as possible.

database

1. Data Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or communicate with our support team. This may include:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Profile content (biography, profile picture, social media links)
  • Preferences and settings (theme selection, notification choices)
  • Support interactions and feedback provided through our platform
cookie

2. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze trends, administer the website, and track users' movements around the platform. These technologies help us:

Session Cookies

Keeps you logged in during your visit and disappears when you close your browser.

Analytical Cookies

Helps us understand how users interact with Wallify IG features.

monitoring

3. Data Usage

Your information is used exclusively to enhance your experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We use the collected data to:

  • check_circle Provide and maintain the Wallify IG service
  • check_circle Personalize your feed and wallpaper recommendations
  • check_circle Communicate technical updates and security alerts
share

4. Third-Party Sharing

We may share information with service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting providers (e.g., AWS), payment processors (e.g., Stripe), and analytics services. These parties are contractually obligated to protect your data.
